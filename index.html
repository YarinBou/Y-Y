Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why do you need to build websites?

    In the current situation where almost every business has a website and promotion on Google or social networks to build a website for a business is a very desirable thing, the very fact that there is a website for a business causes the business exposure on the Internet and produces credibility with the customer. Not every business owner needs to have a website or landing page but there are many business owners and professions that a website is a required and important thing.

  • How to choose a website building company?

    There are many important things to choose a website building company - it is important that there is good chemistry between the parties, whether the company is experienced enough in the type of site the customer is interested in, who is in the company and their experience, whether they strengthen an external or internal designer. Will the site be suitable for promotion, how is the site maintained by the company, does the company have storage or is the storage external.

  • Website building - what do you need to know?

    Important to know 1. Experience 2. Portfolio 3. Responsibility 4. Training on the site 5. What platform is the site built on and is it suitable for the business 6. Who feeds the content? 7. Content Writing - Do not underestimate marketing content writing 8. Optimization for future promotion - construction tailored to promotion needs

  • How much does it cost to build a website?

    Since it is not a tangible physical product like a particular vehicle that can be given a specific price web development is defined as a complex product with endless elements and options and therefore the quote will be complex and there will be no peak price. I can give you a touch of the things that can affect the price of building a website: Are you building the site from 0 or is the site already built and need to repair certain pages or certain areas on the site (sometimes site renovation can be closer in price to building a new site) Which platform would you like the site to be built on (there are several types of platforms) What is the type of site you are interested in building: an image site - with several pages, a store site - that includes clearing product pages, or a landing page - scrolled / long / short. Template - Are you interested in a custom design for you and therefore need to use a professional designer who will understand the business and how the site should look or you are interested in a template site and you have no problem that it will be similar to other sites on the Internet Development of special components - Do you need special plugins or special needs on the site or do you need a basic site Does the person who builds the site know and are experienced in preparing the site for future promotion on Google - one of the most significant parts in building the site and its budget These are the questions you need to ask yourself before talking to a web design company in order to get as accurate a quote as possible.

  • What are the platforms for building a website?

    There are a huge variety of promotion platforms - each customer has a different type of platform tailored to him - for most customers the best platform is WordPress - open source, can be promoted quickly and easily and there are lots and lots of pluses in WordPress compared to the other platforms but not all here. There are other platforms like Shopify, Weeks and more that have customers that are more suitable for them both financially and in terms of convenience.
